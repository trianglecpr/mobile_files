If you have six or more participants, Triangle CPR would be glad to bring this class to you. If the class is being offered at your location, the CPR content can be adjusted to meet the age population you serve.

Note

If you are in or entering the healthcare or dental field, please see Basic Life Support for the Healthcare Provider. This class, Heartsaver CPR and First Aid, is for lay-rescuers, and unlikely to meet the requirements of those in the medical industry.

Heartsaver CPR & First Aid

This class includes comprehensive CPR training as well as medical and injury emergencies most common to adults. This is a general first aid class and is ideal for those who need certification or for personal knowledge.

Heartsaver CPR & Pediatric First Aid

This class includes comprehensive CPR training as well as medical and injury emergencies most common to children infancy until adolescents. This class includes ways to prevent injury as well as how to meet the unique needs of a child who is sick or injured. This is ideal for daycare workers, nannies and parents.

CPR topics for both classes include

  • Adult, Child and Infant CPR
  • Foreign Body Airway Obstruction
  • Use of a barrier device
  • Use of an AED

First Aid topics for both classes include:

  • First Aid basics
  • Medical Emergencies
  • Injury Emergencies
  • Environmental Emergencies
