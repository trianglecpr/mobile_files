From Old Chapel Hill Road (NC Highway 54/NC-54)

  • Head north on Farrington Road

  • In 2.25 miles Pine Cone Drive will be on your left just past the fire station.

From Chapel Hill Blvd (15/501)

  • Turn onto SW Durham Drive

  • In approximately 1 mile it will become Farrington Road

  • In 0.2 miles Pine Cone Drive will be on your right.

  • Take an immediate left into Five Oaks Office Park
