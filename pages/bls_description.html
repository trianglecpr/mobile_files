BLS Course Description

This CPR course is designed for those in or entering the medical profession.

Triangle CPR provides a high-quality training session and a learning-friendly atmosphere. Maintaining a low instructor/student ratio allows for individual attention and support as needed.

Each student will receive an American Heart Association certification card upon successful completion of the course.

Basic Life Support for the Healthcare Provider is commonly required for all medical and dental professionals. This would include those entering CNA or dental hygienist/assistant training.

Triangle CPR provides a learning friendly environment and low student/manikin ratio to ensure ample hands on practice.

Topics include

  • Adult, Child and Infant CPR (1 and 2 rescuer)
  • Foreign Body Airway Obstruction
  • Respiratory arrest
  • Use of a Bag Valve Mask
  • AED training

Each student will receive an American Heart Association certification card upon successful completion of the course.
