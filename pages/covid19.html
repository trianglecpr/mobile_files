COVID-19 Statement

We are still open.
Face coverings will be required in all classes.

We have relaxed our rescheduling policy. If you have any signs and/or symptoms of illness, please reschedule.

Beyond the regulations, no one wants to be part of the problem. Measures taken:

  • We thoroughly wipe down all equipment and surfaces between classes (including door knobs, bathroom fixtures, etc.). We practice using a 1:1 student to manikin ratio. There is no direct mouth to manikin contact.

  • Latex-free gloves are provided when needed and/or upon request as is soap and hand sanitizer.

  • If you come to class with bad cough and/or clearly feverish, you will be asked to reschedule.

Obviously we may have to make adjustments as regulations change. We will keep you informed as we receive new information.
