Heartsaver CPR

This is the CPR course designed for those who are not medical professionals, but are required to maintain CPR certification or simply for personal knowledge.

This class is ideal for the lay-rescuer who need to respond in the event of an emergency, but are not in the healthcare field. This would include personal trainers, construction workers, security guards, teachers and other non-medical professionals.

Triangle CPR provides a learning friendly environment and low student/manikin ratio to ensure ample hands on practice.

Topics include:

  • Adult, Child and Infant CPR*
  • Foreign Body Airway Obstruction
  • Use of a barrier device
  • Use of an AED

Each student will receive an American Heart Association certification card upon successful completion of the course. * If the class is being offered at your location, the content can be focused exclusively on age population you serve.

CPR Where You Are

If you have six or more participants, Triangle CPR would be glad to bring this class to you.

Note

If you are in or entering the healthcare or dental field, please see Basic Life Support for the Healthcare Provider. This class, Heartsaver CPR, is for lay-rescuers, and unlikely to meet the requirements of those in the medical industry.
