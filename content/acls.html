Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS)

is for the practitioner who treats cardiac arrest and peri-arrest conditions in the adult population.

Topics include:
  • Airway management
  • Adult Basic life support and AED use
  • Management of acute coronary syndromes (ACS) and stroke
  • Recognition and early management of peri-arrest conditions such as symptomatic bradycardia and tachycardia
  • Recognition and early management of respiratory and cardiac arrest
  • Management of post cardiac arrest
  • Related pharmacology

Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS)

is for the practitioner who treats cardiac arrest and peri-arrest conditions in the pediatric population.

Topics include:

  • 1- and 2-rescuer child/Infant CPR and AED use
  • Cardiac, respiratory and shock cases
  • Management of respiratory emergencies
  • Resuscitation team concept
  • Rhythm disturbances and electrical therapy
  • Systematic Approach to Pediatric Assessment
  • Related pharmacology
