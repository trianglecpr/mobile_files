From Durham or 540

  • Take I40 East to exit 290. Turn right onto HWY 54 (Chapel Hill Road)

  • Take the first possible right. This entrance to the office park is located directly across from the Hampton Inn. (Please be sure to stay on I40 and not accidentally branch off onto Wade Avenue. 290 will be the next exit.)

  • Triangle CPR is located in the corner of the L-shaped town homes. You will not be able to see the sign for Triangle CPR from the parking lot. Triangle CPR is not affiliated with the company located below with a similar name.

From Raleigh

  • Take I40 West to exit 290. Turn left onto HWY 54 (Chapel Hill Road) crossing the bridge over I40.

  • Take the first possible right. This entrance to the office park is located directly across from the Hampton Inn.

  • Triangle CPR is located in the corner of the L-shaped town homes. You will not be able to see the sign for Triangle CPR from the parking lot. (If you take the Wade Avenue extension, you will come out onto I40 one exit too far west.) Triangle CPR is not affiliated with the company located below with a similar name.
