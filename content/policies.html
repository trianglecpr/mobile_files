Policies

Classes conducted at your location:

Triangle CPR is glad to come to facilities with a minimum for six participants. Classes cancelled/rescheduled with less than 48 hours notice will be charged for the minimal number. This amount cannot be applied to a future class.

We at Triangle CPR understand that unexpected problems can arise, and your staff may need to finish with a patient. We will always do our best to work with you. A prolonged delay, however, may cause the instructor to be late to any further engagement. If the group is not ready within 20 minutes after the scheduled time, the instructor may consider that the class has been cancelled by the facility. The minimum charge will apply.

Please do not bring young children to class.

Individuals coming to Triangle CPR:

Office hours are from 9am—5pm, Monday—Friday. When the class begins outside these hours, the door may not open until 20 minutes prior to class. Class will begin promptly as scheduled. Late arrivals may need to reschedule. This will be at the sole discretion of the instructor. If you are having difficulty finding the location, please call 919 851 4380 before the class begins. We will be glad to direct you.

Please do not bring young children to class.

Refund policy

At Triangle CPR, we understand that the demands of your schedule can lead to a sudden change of plans. You will always be able to reschedule your class without penalty.

If you find you need to reschedule:

  • You will always be able to reschedule your class without penalty.

  • Registration can be rescheduled a maximum of two times before incuring additional fees.

If you find you need to cancel:

  • No refunds will be granted if cancellation is requested within 48 hours of the class.
  • No refunds will be granted for online classes once key code is activated. Funds will not transfer to any other class.
  • If you miss class without calling, there is no refund. You can reschedule only for an existing class.
  • To avoid confusion and provide time/date information, all refund requests must be sent in writing to info@trianglecpr.com

If a book has been sent, the actual cost of the book and postage will be deducted from the original amount.
